Welcome to Joseph's Blog

The Story of Joseph

Joseph pursued his architectural studies in the United States, where he gained both theoretical knowledge and hands-on experience in modern architecture. However, his heart was set on bringing innovative designs to his home country, Cameroon.

"Architecture is more than just buildings; it's about creating spaces that inspire and uplift people." - Joseph

Back to Cameroon

After graduating, Joseph made a bold decision. Instead of staying in the USA where opportunities seemed endless, he chose to return to Cameroon. His goal was clear: to start a design studio that would blend modern architectural principles with local traditions and styles.

Ndode Designs is Born

NADCO was originally established in Maryland, USA, in 2005. In 2008, Joseph decided to return home to his native Cameroon.Following difficulties in transferring the incorporation of Nadco to Cameroon,Joseph decided to take on a different part;in 2010 Ndode design studio was born.

Since then,the studio has been involved in a wide range of projects, including hotels, residences, schools, and healthcare facilities. Ndode Designs is a truly multifaceted studio that seeks, above all, to meet the specific needs of its clients.

Success and Recognition

Ndode Designs has gained recognition not just in Cameroon but internationally as well. Joseph's commitment to his vision and his dedication to quality have made his studio a success. Clients appreciate the innovative approach and personal touch that Joseph brings to each project.

About Joseph

Joseph is a talented architect with a passion for sustainable design. He studied in the USA and gained experience working with top architectural firms before returning to Cameroon. His mission is to bring world-class design to his home country while celebrating local culture and materials.

Ndode Designs

Founded by Joseph,Ndode Designs is a cutting-edge design studio based in Cameroon. Specializing in innovative and eco-friendly architectural solutions, the studio is transforming the way people think about space and design.
